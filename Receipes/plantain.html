A bowl of Plantain

Plantains are a versatile and delicious fruit that can be used in a variety of dishes. They are rich in potassium and fiber, making them a healthy choice.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe plantains
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of pepper
  • 1 tsp of garlic powder
  • 1 tsp of onion powder
  • 1 tsp of paprika
  • 1 tsp of cumin
  • 1 tsp of chili powder
  • 1 tsp of oregano
  • 1 tsp of thyme
  • 1 tsp of rosemary
  • 1 tsp of basil
  • 1 tsp of parsley
  • 1 tsp of cilantro
  • 1 tsp of dill
  • 1 tsp of sage
  • 1 tsp of tarragon
  • 1 tsp of marjoram
  • 1 tsp of fennel
  • 1 tsp of cardamom
  • 1 tsp of nutmeg
  • 1 tsp of cinnamon
  • 1 tsp of allspice

Steps

  1. Peel the plantains and slice them into thin rounds.
  2. In a bowl, combine the olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, cumin, chili powder, oregano, thyme, rosemary, basil, parsley, cilantro, dill, sage, tarragon, marjoram, fennel, cardamom, nutmeg, cinnamon and allspice.
  3. Add the plantain slices to the bowl and coat them with the marinade.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and add the plantain slices.
  5. Cook for 3-4 minutes on each side or until golden brown.
  6. Remove from heat and serve with your favorite sides.
