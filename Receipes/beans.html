A bowl of Beans

Beans are a great source of protein and fiber. They are also low in fat and can be used in a variety of dishes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of beans
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of pepper
  • 1 tsp of garlic powder
  • 1 tsp of onion powder
  • 1 tsp of cumin
  • 1 tsp of chili powder
  • 1 tsp of paprika
  • 1 tsp of oregano
  • 1 tsp of thyme
  • 1 tsp of rosemary
  • 1 tsp of basil
  • 1 tsp of parsley
  • 1 tsp of cilantro
  • 1 tsp of dill
  • 1 tsp of sage
  • 1 tsp of tarragon
  • 1 tsp of marjoram
  • 1 tsp of fennel
  • 1 tsp of cardamom
  • 1 tsp of nutmeg
  • 1 tsp of cinnamon
  • 1 tsp of allspice

    • steps

    1. Soak the beans in water overnight.
    2. Drain the beans and rinse them under cold water.
    3. In a large pot, combine the beans, water, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, cumin, chili powder, paprika, oregano, thyme, rosemary, basil, parsley, cilantro, dill, sage, tarragon, marjoram, fennel, cardamom, nutmeg, cinnamon and allspice.
    4. Bring to a boil over high heat.
    5. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 1-2 hours or until the beans are tender.
