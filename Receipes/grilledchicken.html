A bowl of Grilled Chicken

Grilled chicken is a healthy and delicious option for any meal. It is low in fat and high in protein, making it a great choice for those looking to eat healthier.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of chicken breast
  • 2 tbsp of olive oil
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of pepper
  • 1 tsp of garlic powder
  • 1 tsp of onion powder
  • 1 tsp of paprika
  • 1 tsp of cumin
  • 1 tsp of chili powder
  • 1 tsp of oregano
  • 1 tsp of thyme
  • 1 tsp of rosemary
  • 1 tsp of basil
  • 1 tsp of parsley
  • 1 tsp of cilantro
  • 1 tsp of dill
  • 1 tsp of sage
  • 1 tsp of tarragon
  • 1 tsp of marjoram
  • 1 tsp of fennel
  • 1 tsp of cardamom
  • 1 tsp of nutmeg
  • 1 tsp of cinnamon
  • 1 tsp of allspice

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, combine the olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, cumin, chili powder, oregano, thyme, rosemary, basil, parsley, cilantro, dill, sage, tarragon, marjoram, fennel, cardamom, nutmeg, cinnamon and allspice.
  3. Add the chicken breast to the bowl and coat it with the marinade.
  4. Place the chicken on the grill and cook for 6-8 minutes on each side or until the internal temperature reaches 165Â°F.
  5. Remove from the grill and let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
  6. Serve with your favorite sides.
